Ownership
Manifesto

Not your KEYS
Not your COINS

Irresponsible custodians have let us down. Ownership is undermined by trusting solely centralised custodians. We have to take it back in our hands as individuals, groups and projects.

Each owner is
SOVEREIGN

Individuals need to be empowered. We have to coordinate action and protect the sovereignty of individual owners.

We don't trust
We VERIFY

We do not trust even the best of actors. We are skeptics that demand proof. We are only satisfied with on-chain evidence.

We speak up,
then we BUILD

We need to gather awareness against blatant misuse of authority. Equally, we need to build better systems for custody.

We TRANSITION
off custody

Self-custody is not new. It is an innately human idea. We have evolved to protect what we own. Web3 needs to make systems simple enough for humanity. We build, collaborate, onboard and help anyone transitioning.

Are you with us?
